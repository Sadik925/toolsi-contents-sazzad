Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is a UTM Builder?

A UTM Builder is a tool that lets you create trackable URLs by appending UTM parameters, making it easier to analyze where your traffic is coming from.

Why should I use UTM parameters?

Using UTM parameters helps you identify which marketing campaigns drive traffic and conversions, providing valuable insights in tools like Google Analytics.

What are the key UTM parameters?

The main UTM parameters are source, medium, campaign, term, and content. These allow you to track the origin and details of the traffic accurately.
