QR Code Decoder

Easily decode QR codes from images or webcam to reveal text, URLs, or data instantly.

QR Code Decoder is a simple and fast online tool that lets you extract information encoded in any QR code. Just upload your image or use your webcam to scan a code, and the tool will reveal the embedded content in seconds.

This tool supports all standard QR formats and works perfectly on all modern browsers and devices. Whether it's a link, text, contact info, or a payment request, decode and view it instantly with zero hassle and no need for extra apps.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How does the QR Code Decoder work?

It analyzes the QR code image using your browser and extracts the encoded data, whether it's text, a URL, or other content.

Do I need to install any software?

No. This is a browser-based tool, so you don't need to download or install anything to decode QR codes.

Can I use my webcam to scan a QR code?

Yes. You can allow webcam access and scan QR codes in real-time using your camera directly within the tool.

Is my data secure while using this tool?

Yes. The QR code decoding happens entirely on your device and nothing is uploaded to any server, ensuring complete privacy.
